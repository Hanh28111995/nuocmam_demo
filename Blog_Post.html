Banh Khot is a specialty dish in the East of Vietnam and has been available in many cities. Wanting a Banh Khot dish to be good, besides having the right batter need to have the right kind of seasoned fish sauce. However, not everyone knows how to make a delicous fish sauce for these dipping pancakes. If at the weekend you are planning to win some heart with new dishes for your family, make these Banh Khot, and you should immediately pocket these "tips" on how to make the delicious fish sauce below of Nuoc Mam Hoang Gia.

Want Banh Khot to be good need to have a good fish sauce

Prepare the ingredients for the Fish Sauce

To make a Banh Khot fish sauce that tasted exactly like in the East, you need to prepare the following ingredients:

Delicious fish sauce: 5 – 6 coffee table spoon
Carrot: 1 piece
Garlic: 1 piece
Red chilli pepper: 2 – 3  
Lime: 2 – 3 according to the family’s taste
Rice vinegar: 100ml
Green papaya or ripe, depending on the right texture: 200gr
White radish: 1
Cool boiled water: 100ml
Other seasoning: MGS, salt,…

Anyone can make fish sauce for banh khot dipping on the first try

All the ingredients above are used for making Fish sauce for a family of 4-6 people. You can adjust the right ratio that is suitable according to the amount of servings and also your family’s taste.


Step by step on how to make Banh Khot’s fish sauce

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

After buying the papaya, cut both of the side stem so the resin can leak all out. Wash the papaya thoroughly with clean water, chop in 4 or 6 then remove the (Chop before remove the peel will make it easier for you to prepare it one by one). After remove the peel for each piece you drop each pieces in salted diluted water.
Soak it for about 20 mins then get them out. This will help the papaya keep the freshness and crispiness.

Carrots and white radish you cut and peel all of them.

Peel your garlic and mince all of them.

Chillies remove the stem, seeds, chopped or finely minced.

Chop the lime in half, remove seeds and squeeze the juice.

Step 2: Make the side pickles for the pancakes and the sauce

The papaya you can finely mince to your size, shape of liking, the size need to be consumable (also put them in cold ice water to keep the crispy texture of the papaya). Keep them in stored when you need them, get them out to dry. 

Carrots and white radish would need to be prepare the same. 

Prepare a big bowl, put in papaya, carrot and white radish. Adding vinegar, squeeze all of the vegetables lightly. with vinegar then rinse all of then and let to dry.

Then take another bowl, put in the sugar, salt, vinegar and a little MSG, mix until all ingredients are combine. 

Put the mixture that you just created in the papaya bowl, radishes and carrots, mix then leave them for about from 20 – 30 minutes. 

Step 3: Preparing the fish sauce.

You put in sugar, fish sauce, lime juice and cool boiled water into a bowl, mix until sugar is dissolved (if it’s rock sugar, you need to mince the rock sugar finely before using). you can taste test before adding more fish sauce or sugar and lime to fit your taste.

Put on a stove and gently boil the mixture until reach the right amount of warmth, then take it down.

Adding garlic, mince chillies and gently miz then finish.

Want Banh Khot to be good need to have a good fish sauce

When using, you can put in the side pickles into the fish sauce or can leave it separately along with the other vegetables. White radish in the pickles can be optional (depending on the family taste).

Wanting the banh khot fish sauce to be good your can use a great traditional fish sauce. This recipe can not only use for banh khot dipping but can also be perfect for banh xeo. You can use this for many dishes.
